# FBChat-v2 Project Code of Conduct

## Maqsad (Purpose)

FBChat-v2 project sab contributors, users aur community members ke liye ek welcoming aur inclusive environment provide karne ke liye committed hai. Ye Code of Conduct hamare expectations outline karta hai respectful aur collaborative behavior ke liye is project mein.

## Hamare Principles

1. **Izzat (Respect):** Doosron ke saath kindness, consideration aur empathy se pesh aao. Har koi yahan seekhne aur contribute karne aaya hai, chahe background ya experience kuch bhi ho.

2. **Inclusive Mahaul (Inclusivity):** Diversity ko apnao aur har background ke participants ka swagat karo. Hum alag-alag perspectives aur experiences ko encourage karte hain taaki community enrich ho.

3. **Milkar Kaam Karna (Collaboration):** Cooperation aur teamwork ki bhavna rakhlo. Apna knowledge share karo, doosron ki madad karo aur common goals achieve karne ke liye milkar kaam karo.

4. **Professionalism:** Professional tarike se kaam karo. Offensive language, harassment ya kisi bhi tarah ke disruptive behavior se bacho.

## Specific Guidelines

- **Baat-Cheet (Communication):** Discussions, comments aur interactions mein apni language ka dhyan rakho. Personal attacks, discrimination ya offensive content se bacho.

- **Feedback:** Constructive aur respectful feedback do. Individuals ke bajay content aur ideas par focus karo.

- **Inclusive Hona:** Ensure karo ki discussions aur decisions inclusive hon. Kisi bhi individual ya group ko unki characteristics ki wajah se exclude ya marginalize mat karo.

- **Code of Conduct Violations:** Agar koi is Code of Conduct ka violation kare toh project maintainers ko report karo. Violations ko turant aur appropriately address kiya jaega.

## Enforcement

Abusive, harassing, ya kisi bhi tarah ke unacceptable behavior ki reports project team ko contact karke ki ja sakti hain. Project team sabhi complaints review karega aur investigate karega aur circumstances ke hisaab se zaroorat padne par respond karega. Project team reporter ki confidentiality maintain karne ke liye obligated hai.

Is Code of Conduct ka violation karne wale participants ko project community se temporary ya permanent expulsion ka samna karna pad sakta hai.

## Conclusion

FBChat-v2 Project mein participate karke, tum is Code of Conduct ka paalan karne se agree karte ho. Hum ek positive aur inclusive community foster karne ke liye dedicated hain, aur tumhara cooperation is goal ko achieve karne ke liye zaroorat hai.
